Travel Info

Guarulhos (GRU) is the largest airport in Brazil and handles most international flights, it is located 31 km from São Paulo city center.

Congonhas (CGH) is a more centrally located airport (8 km), but it only operates domestic flights. Therefore, if your trip involves domestic flights or if you have a connection through another airport in Brazil, it could be a convenient option.

Another, more distant, alternative is Campinas-Viracopos (VCP) (97 km), which offers flights to some destinations outside Brazil (Portugal, Spain, the USA, Argentina, Uruguay, Paraguay) and has direct bus service to the Tietê Bus Terminal in São Paulo (company name: VB Transportes).

Travel times to the city from each airport to downtown São Paulo under typical traffic conditions: GRU: 45–90 min; CGH: 20–40 min; VCP: 1h30–2h.

If you are arriving to Guarulhos airport (GRU), you can book a transfer service from to the São Paulo city, either to the conference venue or to your hotel. Check details here: Ever Transfer.
