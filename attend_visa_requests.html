Visa Requests

 

Citizens of Argentina, Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Paraguay, Peru, Uruguay, and Venezuela do not need a visa to enter Brazil.

Citizens traveling with passports from Australia, Canada, and the United States visiting Brazil for tourism and business will be subject to visa requirements for entry into Brazilian territory starting from April 10th, 2025. These citizens can require e-Visas through the portal https://brazil.vfsevisa.com/.

Please check visa requirement for citizens of other countries here. If you require a visa to enter Brazil, please consult the website of the embassy of Brazil in your home country. Here is a list of diplomatic missions of Brazil.
