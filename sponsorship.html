Partner with ISSRE 2025

Showcase Your Brand at the Forefront of Software Reliability

The IEEE International Symposium on Software Reliability Engineering (ISSRE) is the premier conference in the field, bringing together top-tier researchers, industry leaders, and practitioners from around the world.

ISSRE 2025 will take place in Brazil and promises to be a vibrant and dynamic event, offering sponsors a unique opportunity to engage with an elite international audience deeply invested in innovation, reliability, and software excellence.

By sponsoring ISSRE, your organization will:

  • Be recognized as a leader in supporting cutting-edge research and education in software reliability.
  • Gain high-visibility brand exposure across our global channels and onsite during the conference.
  • Connect with potential collaborators, clients, and top talent from both academia and industry.

Whether your goal is to boost brand recognition, support technical excellence, or recruit high-quality professionals, ISSRE offers a variety of sponsorship packages to match your objectives.
